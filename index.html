Paw's outdoors

Paw's outdoors is a pet care provider with a caring touch. Paw's outdoors offers dog walking, pet welfare checks and puppy play sessions. Trained and accredited to work with dogs, train puppies and help with recall and lead pulling. Qualified to peform dog CPR and deliver aid to choking dogs.

Meet Maria

I am a real animal lover with a wealth of experience in care. Following from maternity leave & eight years of experience in the care industry I am now turning my focus to Paw's Outdoors, my new pet care venture. Maria has two beautiful dogs and has previously cared for a real menagarie of animals from snakes, hamsters and owls to ferrets and birds.
