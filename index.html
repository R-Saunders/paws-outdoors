Paw's outdoors

Paw's outdoors is my passion, I love animals all shapes and sizes and believe they all deserve to be looked after with love. In my family we have two beautiful dogs and prior to this I have cared for many other animals from snakes, owls and hamsters to ferrets, domestic birds and others. Following a gap for maternity leave I am now moving on from providing top quality care for eight years with award winning care providers and into the pet care industry with Paw's Outdoors.
